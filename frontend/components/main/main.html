What is DeliveryHeatMaps designed for?

DeliveryHeatMap is a web service for people who has an internet shop with delivery. With help of DeliveryHeatMap you can build a "heat map" of all your deliveries and explore in on map. This type of representation makes it so easy to analyze your business, see the most popular places in your city, find a way to improve your ads and to understand better the demand situation on the store.
